Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Dry Fruits

Recipe

  1. First, curdle the milk using lemon juice or vinegar to make chenna and strain the water.
  2. Knead the chenna until smooth and shape it into small flat balls.
  3. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup, then cook the balls in it until they turn soft and spongy.
  4. In another pan, simmer milk with sugar, cardamom, saffron, and nuts to make rabdi.
  5. Finally, add the cooked balls to the rabdi and refrigerate before serving.
